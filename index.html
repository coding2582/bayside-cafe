Our Story

"We wanted to prove vegan food could be the comfort food you crave, not the thing you settle for."

Jasper and Ryan opened The Bayside in August 2020, launching from a tiny space on Hewitt Avenue during a genuinely uncertain time. Word got out fast, so they moved into a larger space, kept the original dragon murals on the walls, and made it their own. Today it's a full vegan diner and lounge where the burgers stack high and the door's open to everyone.

FAQ

Questions people ask us.

Everything worth knowing before your first visit to Everett's all-vegan diner and lounge.

Is The Bayside Cafe fully vegan?

Yes. Every item on the menu is 100% plant-based, from the burgers and hand-breaded chick'n to the nacho cheese sauce. No animal products, no exceptions.

Where is The Bayside Cafe located?

2913 W Marine View Dr, Everett, WA 98201. Look for the black building with the octopus signs on West Marine View Drive.

What are The Bayside Cafe's hours?

Monday–Saturday 11am–1am, and Sunday 11am–3pm. Open seven days a week.

Do you offer takeout or delivery?

Both. Call ahead for takeout, or order delivery through Uber Eats using the "Order Online" button on this page.

Is there parking near The Bayside Cafe?

Yes, street parking is available right outside on West Marine View Drive.

Do you serve alcohol?

We do. Vegan beer is on tap alongside the food menu.

What makes The Bayside Cafe different from other vegan restaurants?

Everything is made from scratch, including the hand-breaded chick'n, house-made sauces, and a nacho cheese sauce we're especially proud of. It's comfort food first, vegan second, built for anyone who just wants a good burger.
